# Employee Benefits Guide

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Applies To:** All full-time employees of Acme Corp
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department

## 1. Overview

Acme Corp is committed to providing a comprehensive benefits package that supports the health, financial security, and overall well-being of our employees and their families. This guide details all available benefits and enrollment information.

## 2. Health Insurance

Acme Corp offers three health insurance tiers through our provider, BlueCross National. Eligibility begins on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment.

### 2.2 Enrollment
- **Open Enrollment:** November 1-30 each year for the following calendar year.
- **Life Events:** Marriage, birth/adoption, divorce, or loss of other coverage triggers a 30-day special enrollment window.
- **New Hires:** Must enroll within 30 days of start date. If no selection is made, the Basic plan (employee-only) is assigned by default.

## 3. Dental Insurance

All health plan enrollees automatically receive dental coverage at no additional cost.

- **Preventive Care:** 100% covered (cleanings, exams, X-rays) -- 2 visits per year
- **Basic Procedures:** 80% covered (fillings, extractions, root canals)
- **Major Procedures:** 50% covered (crowns, bridges, implants)
- **Orthodontia:** 50% covered up to $2,000 lifetime maximum (dependents under 19 only)
- **Annual Maximum:** $2,000 per person

## 4. Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is included with all health plans.

- **Eye Exam:** $10 copay, once per year
- **Eyeglass Frames:** $150 allowance every 2 years
- **Lenses:** Covered in full (standard lenses) every year
- **Contact Lenses:** $150 allowance per year (in lieu of frames)
- **LASIK Discount:** 15% off at participating providers

## 5. 401(k) Retirement Plan

Acme Corp offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan through Fidelity Investments.

- **Eligibility:** Immediately upon hire.
- **Employee Contribution:** Up to the IRS annual limit ($23,500 for 2025; $31,000 if age 50+).
- **Company Match:** Acme Corp matches **100% of the first 4%** of salary contributed.
- **Vesting:** Company match is vested on a 3-year graded schedule:
  - Year 1: 33%
  - Year 2: 66%
  - Year 3: 100%
- **Investment Options:** 25+ mutual funds, target-date funds, and a self-directed brokerage window.
- **Loans:** Employees may borrow up to 50% of vested balance (max $50,000) with a 5-year repayment term.

## 6. Life Insurance

- **Basic Life Insurance:** **2x annual base salary**, paid by Acme Corp (no cost to employee).
- **Maximum Coverage:** $500,000.
- **Supplemental Life:** Employees may purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary. Rates vary by age.
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D):** Equal to basic life insurance amount, paid by Acme Corp.
- **Dependent Life:** Optional coverage available for spouse ($50,000 max) and children ($10,000 max).

## 7. Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

- **Healthcare FSA:** Contribute up to $3,300 pre-tax per year for eligible medical expenses.
- **Dependent Care FSA:** Contribute up to $5,000 pre-tax per year for childcare or elder care expenses.
- **Use-it-or-lose-it:** FSA funds must be used by December 31. A $610 rollover is permitted for Healthcare FSA only.

## 8. Health Savings Account (HSA)

Available to employees enrolled in the Basic health plan (high-deductible plan).

- **Individual Contribution Limit:** $4,300 per year (2025).
- **Family Contribution Limit:** $8,550 per year (2025).
- **Acme Corp Contribution:** $500/year (individual) or $1,000/year (family), deposited quarterly.
- **Funds Roll Over:** HSA balances carry over year to year with no expiration.
- **Investment:** Balances over $1,000 can be invested in mutual funds.

## 9. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Acme Corp provides a confidential Employee Assistance Program at no cost to all employees and their household members.

- **Counseling:** Up to 6 free sessions per issue per year (in-person, phone, or video).
- **Topics Covered:** Stress, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, substance abuse, grief, financial concerns, legal questions.
- **Work-Life Services:** Childcare referrals, elder care resources, legal consultations, financial planning.
- **24/7 Hotline:** 1-800-555-HELP (available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week).
- **Confidential:** EAP usage is never reported to Acme Corp. Your privacy is protected.

## 10. Additional Benefits
