# New Hire Onboarding Guide

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Applies To:** All new full-time employees of Acme Corp
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department

## 1. Welcome to Acme Corp

Congratulations on joining Acme Corp! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know during your first 90 days. Our goal is to help you settle in quickly, meet your team, and start contributing with confidence.

## 2. Before Your First Day

Your HR coordinator will send you a welcome email 5 business days before your start date containing:

- **Offer letter** (signed copy for your records)
- **Benefits enrollment link** (must complete within 30 days of start date)
- **Tax forms** (W-4, I-9, state tax withholding)
- **Direct deposit form**
- **Emergency contact form**
- **Parking pass or transit card application** (if applicable)
- **Building access badge photo submission** link

Please complete all paperwork before your first day so we can focus on getting you set up.

## 3. Day 1: Welcome & Setup

### 3.1 Arrival
- **Time:** 9:00 AM (or as arranged with your manager)
- **Location:** Main lobby -- your HR buddy will meet you at reception
- **What to Bring:** Government-issued photo ID (for I-9 verification), voided check or bank details for direct deposit

### 3.2 Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
1. **Welcome session with HR** (30 minutes)
   - Building tour, emergency exits, restroom locations
   - Badge activation and security protocols
   - Review of company policies and employee handbook
2. **IT setup** (60-90 minutes)
   - Receive your laptop and peripherals
   - Account creation: email, Slack, Jira, GitHub, Google Workspace
   - VPN setup and security training (mandatory)
   - Install required software
3. **Meet your manager** (30 minutes)
   - Team overview and your role
   - First-week expectations
   - Introduction to your onboarding buddy

### 3.3 Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
1. **Team lunch** -- your manager will organize a welcome lunch with your immediate team
2. **Workspace setup** -- personalize your desk/office, set up monitors, etc.
3. **Self-paced orientation modules** (on the learning portal):
   - Company history and mission
   - Organizational structure
   - Code of conduct
   - Information security awareness

## 4. Week 1: Getting Oriented

### 4.1 Team Introductions
- Your manager will schedule 1:1 meetings with each team member (15-30 min each).
- Cross-functional introductions with key stakeholders you will work with.

### 4.2 Tools & Systems Training
- **Communication:** Slack channels to join, email etiquette, meeting norms
- **Project Management:** Jira/Asana walkthrough, how to log work
- **Documentation:** Confluence/Notion, where to find what
- **Engineering (if applicable):** Git workflow, CI/CD pipeline, code review process

### 4.3 Onboarding Buddy
Every new hire is paired with an **onboarding buddy** -- an experienced team member who:
- Answers day-to-day questions
- Helps navigate company culture
- Invites you to social events
- Checks in with you regularly during your first 90 days

Your buddy is NOT your manager -- they are a peer who can give you an honest, informal perspective.

## 5. Month 1: Building Foundations

### 5.1 30-Day Check-In
At the end of your first month, your manager will conduct a **30-day check-in** covering:
- How you are feeling about the role and team
- Any questions or concerns
- Initial feedback on your work
- Adjustments needed to your onboarding plan

### 5.2 Goal Setting
Together with your manager, you will set:
- **30-day goals:** Get comfortable with tools, processes, and team dynamics
- **60-day goals:** Start contributing independently to projects
- **90-day goals:** Full productivity, ownership of specific deliverables

### 5.3 Required Training (Complete by Day 30)
- [ ] Information Security Awareness
- [ ] Anti-Harassment Training
- [ ] Data Privacy & Compliance
- [ ] Emergency Procedures
- [ ] Benefits Enrollment (if not completed pre-start)

## 6. Equipment Policy

### 6.1 Standard Equipment
Every new hire receives:
- **Laptop:** MacBook Pro 14" (Engineering/Design) or Dell Latitude (all other departments)
- **Monitor:** 27" 4K display
- **Peripherals:** Keyboard, mouse, headset

### 6.2 Additional Equipment Budget
- **One-time budget:** $500 for additional accessories (ergonomic chair, standing desk converter, webcam, etc.)
- Submit requests through the IT portal within your first 60 days.
- Receipts required for reimbursement.

### 6.3 Equipment Ownership
- All equipment is property of Acme Corp and must be returned upon termination.
- Equipment older than 3 years may be purchased by the employee at fair market value upon replacement.

## 7. Month 2-3: Growing & Contributing

### 7.1 60-Day Check-In
- Review progress against 60-day goals
- Discuss any roadblocks or support needed
- Begin discussing long-term career development

### 7.2 90-Day Review (End of Probation)
The 90-day review is a formal milestone:
- **Performance assessment** against agreed goals
- **Cultural fit** feedback from manager and peers
- **Decision:** Confirmation of employment or extension of probationary period
- **Next steps:** Set Q2/Q3 goals and development plan

### 7.3 Completion
After your 90-day review:
- You are officially off probation
- Full access to all benefits (some benefits like tuition reimbursement require 1 year of employment)
- Eligible for internal job postings and transfers

## 8. Remote Onboarding

For remote employees, the process is adapted:
- Equipment is shipped to your home address 3-5 business days before your start date.
- Day 1 sessions are conducted via video call.
- Virtual team lunches are organized using a food delivery credit ($30).
- Your onboarding buddy will schedule daily 15-minute check-ins during Week 1.
- You are invited to visit the office during the next quarterly all-hands (travel covered by Acme Corp).

## 10. FAQ

**Q: When do I get my first paycheck?**
A: Payday is the 15th and last day of each month. Your first paycheck will be prorated based on your start date.

**Q: Can I change my benefits elections after enrollment?**
A: Only during Open Enrollment (November 1-30) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event.

**Q: What if I need equipment that is not on the standard list?**
A: Submit a request through the IT portal with a business justification. Non-standard equipment requires manager approval.

**Q: Who do I contact if I have a problem with my manager?**
A: Reach out to HR (hr@acmecorp.com) or use the anonymous Employee Assistance Hotline (1-800-555-HELP).
