# Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Applies To:** All full-time employees of Acme Corp
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources Department

## 1. Overview

Acme Corp embraces a hybrid work model that combines the benefits of in-person collaboration with the flexibility of remote work. This policy outlines expectations, eligibility, and support available to all employees.

## 2. Work Model

### 2.1 Hybrid Model (Default)
The standard work arrangement for most positions:
- **In-office days:** 3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday)
- **Remote days:** 2 days per week (Monday and Friday)
- **Flexibility:** With manager approval, employees may swap their remote/office days within the same week.

### 2.2 Fully Remote
Some positions are designated as **fully remote** (indicated in the job description). Fully remote employees:
- Have no mandatory in-office days
- Must visit the office for quarterly all-hands meetings (travel covered)
- Must be located within the continental United States
- Follow the same core hours as hybrid employees

### 2.3 Fully On-Site
Certain roles (facilities, lab work, reception) require full-time on-site presence. These roles are marked as on-site only in the job description and are not eligible for remote work.

## 3. Core Hours

Regardless of location or time zone, all employees must be available during **core hours**:

- **Core Hours:** 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM Eastern Time
- This applies to all US time zones. For example:
  - Pacific Time: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  - Mountain Time: 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  - Central Time: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  - Eastern Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

**What "available" means:**
- Responsive on Slack (within 15 minutes for direct messages)
- Able to join video calls with short notice (within 30 minutes)
- Status accurately reflects your availability (green = available, yellow = away, red = do not disturb)

Outside of core hours, employees may set their own schedule as long as they complete their standard 40 hours per week.

## 4. Home Office Setup

### 4.1 One-Time Stipend
All employees (hybrid and fully remote) receive a **$500 one-time home office stipend** upon hire to set up a productive workspace. Eligible purchases include:
- Desk and ergonomic chair
- Monitor stand or laptop stand
- Webcam and lighting
- Keyboard and mouse
- Noise-cancelling headphones
- Cable management and desk accessories

### 4.2 Monthly Internet Stipend
- **Amount:** $50 per month for all hybrid and fully remote employees
- **Purpose:** Offset home internet costs
- **Payment:** Added to monthly paycheck (taxable)
- **No receipts required**

### 4.3 Workspace Requirements
Employees working remotely must have:
- A dedicated workspace with a door that can close (for confidential meetings)
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps download / 5 Mbps upload)
- A quiet environment suitable for video calls
- Adequate lighting for video meetings

## 5. Communication Expectations

### 5.1 Slack
- **Primary communication tool** for real-time messaging
- Keep your status updated throughout the day
- Use threads for discussions to keep channels organized
- Respond to direct messages within 15 minutes during core hours
- Use @channel and @here sparingly -- only for urgent matters

### 5.2 Video Calls
- **Camera on** for all team meetings and 1:1s (unless bandwidth issues)
- Use virtual backgrounds if your home environment is distracting
- Mute when not speaking in large meetings
- All meetings must have a calendar invite with a video link

### 5.3 Async Communication
- Document decisions in Confluence/Notion, not just Slack
- Use Loom or recorded Slack huddles for updates that don't need a live meeting
- Respect time zones -- don't expect immediate responses outside core hours

### 5.4 Meetings
- **No-meeting Fridays:** No recurring meetings on Fridays (async catch-ups encouraged)
- All meetings should have an agenda shared at least 1 hour before
- Meetings longer than 30 minutes should have a note-taker assigned
- Default meeting length: 25 minutes (not 30) to allow bio breaks

## 6. Quarterly All-Hands

All employees (including fully remote) are expected to attend the **quarterly all-hands** in person:
- **Frequency:** 4 times per year (January, April, July, October)
- **Duration:** 2 days (typically Tuesday-Wednesday)
- **Location:** Acme Corp headquarters (New York)
- **Travel:** Acme Corp covers round-trip airfare, hotel (up to $250/night), and a $75/day meal per diem
- **Booking:** Use the corporate travel portal or submit receipts for reimbursement within 30 days

## 7. Security & Compliance

### 7.1 VPN
- **Required** when accessing company systems from any network outside the office
- Use the company-provided VPN client at all times when working remotely
- Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN (coffee shops, airports, hotels)

### 7.2 Device Security
- Enable full-disk encryption on your laptop
- Use the company-provided password manager
- Lock your screen when stepping away
- Do not allow family members to use your work devices

### 7.3 Confidential Information
- Do not discuss confidential business matters in public spaces
- Use headphones for all video calls when in shared spaces
- Ensure your screen is not visible to others when working with sensitive data

## 8. International Remote Work

Employees who wish to work from outside the United States temporarily:
- **Maximum duration:** 4 weeks per calendar year
- **Approval required:** Manager + HR approval at least 2 weeks in advance
- **Restrictions:** Cannot work from countries under US sanctions
- **Tax implications:** Employee is responsible for understanding and complying with local tax obligations
- **Time zones:** Must still be available during core hours (may require adjusted schedule)

## 9. Performance & Accountability

Remote and hybrid employees are held to the same performance standards as on-site employees. Performance is measured by:
- Deliverables and project outcomes (not hours logged)
- Responsiveness and collaboration
- Participation in team activities and meetings
- Manager feedback through regular 1:1s

If a remote employee consistently fails to meet performance expectations or communication standards, the manager may require increased in-office days as a corrective measure.

## 10. Equipment & Expenses

### 10.1 Company-Provided Equipment
All employees receive a laptop and standard peripherals (see Onboarding Guide). This equipment must be used for work and returned upon termination.

### 10.2 Expense Reimbursement
Remote employees may expense:
- Co-working space fees (up to $200/month with manager approval)
- Office supplies (paper, pens, printer ink -- up to $50/quarter)
- Ergonomic equipment beyond the initial $500 stipend (requires HR approval)
